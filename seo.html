Frequently asked questions

All your FAQs

1. How long does it take to see SEO results?
The general understanding is that it takes a good SEO strategy to start showing positive results in 3 months. This is because search engines like Google need time to evauluate your website, your content and compare you against others in the same space.
2. Can my business rank first on Google?
Yes, it is possible but you need a great SEO strategy and lots of patience. Ranking on the first page on Google isn't as easy as flipping a switch, otherwise everyone would be there. It takes knowledge of your market, competitors and brand/product fit to stand out. If you offer search engines great value in terms of a digital experience, then you will rank high.
3. Is SEO difficult?
It can be if you don't follow the guidelines. There are thousands of things to consider for SEO, but the main ones are always an excellent performing website with no technical issues, engaging content and user experience.
3. Can I pay Google to rank first in SEO?
No, but you can use paid ads (PPC) to achieve it. Search engines like Google want to serve their users the best, most relevant content. Search engines use thousands of pin points to understand your content and make sure it's best for the person searching for it. Website copy, technical elements and performance are key points that rank websites. PPC is used to show ads on search engines and can be a quicker way to get resulsts over a longer organic SEO strategy, but it too works on serving relevant content. Example: If you sell shoes from a physical store in Manchester, you won't show in the search results for someone in London looking for a shoe store near them.
