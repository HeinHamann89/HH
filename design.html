Frequently asked questions

Your FAQs

1. How long does a design take?
If your brief contains a lot of detail and requirements, designers will need some time to conceptualise it into reality. This could take a week depending on scale.
2. Is graphic design expensive?
Like art, you pay for the quality of the artist. There is no set price but rather an estimation based on your brief's requirements.
3. Who owns the graphics?
You do! We will create them and hand it all over to you once your'e happy. Then you have full ownership of it all. If you want, we can mint them as NFT's and you can track onwership on a blockchain.
